Space Jeopardy Event

  • Naisha Bhandari
  • December 1st, 2023

Today our school held its annual Talent Show and International Dinner! This initiative invited families all over our county and raised over $1,500 for the Marine Toys for Tots Foundation. Accompanied by the amazing dancing and musical performances of Middlesex, our LO-TEMP ADC team hosted a fun “Space Jeopardy” event.

By getting the audience involved, students ranging from high school freshman to seniors and school alumni showed off their space knowledge. LO-TEMP team members wrote 24 space-related questions in 5 original categories: Small Step, Gen Artemis, Quantum-mania, Moon-shot, and Star Stud. Multiple teams composed of 3 students each were featured as the audience learned and played along as a fun and engaging way to expand their space knowledge and awareness.
