Quantum Computing School Challenge

  • Naisha Bhandari
  • December 5th, 2023

Our LO-TEMP team recently hosted a competition at our school, challenging students to obtain the highest score on Quander, a game exercising the fundamentals of Quantum Computing. There were 5 distinct activities prompting users to navigate mazes, learn about relational operators, and practicing simplification. The top 3 competitors received prizes based on mini games completed given 96 hours. Thank you to everyone who participated!

We also sent students these Quantum Computing links and resources for the challenge:

https://quantumchess.net/
https://quantumflytrap.com/
https://quantumzeitgeist.com/10-quantum-games-that-can-help-you-learn-the-field-of-quantum-computing/
https://quantumai.google/cirq
https://qiskit.org/
https://quander.cs.uchicago.edu/
